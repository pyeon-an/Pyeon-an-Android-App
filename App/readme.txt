This Application makes easy to reserve your seat in PC Cafe. 
PC Cafe in Korea needs to pay your PC Cafe money in cash. And to pay that money you must stand up and go to casher or to recharge machine.
That bothered many PC Cafe users due to PC may be turned off, if they don't recharge. 
So we developed Application that can recharge you PC Cafe money on your place by application.

This Application is developed by Android Studio with Kotlion Language. We have used Firebase to control our Databases which is NoSQL type.
